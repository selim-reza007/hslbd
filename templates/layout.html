We are HSLBD

Healthcare Solutions Ltd. is a fully owned member of Ornate and Lyric Business Group. The company has been established to dedicate itself to healthcare products and services, involving import, sales, marketing, distribution and supply through business to business promotion & direct sales, tender participation in private and government Healthcare / Industrial procurement in Bangladesh. The company primarily specializing in Healthcare consumers Products and medical devices, industrial equipment, chemical and regents, and is ready to ensure high quality customer service and support, a very important key to success in this field. The company is managed by highly motivated professionals with expert knowledge and specialization in modern healthcare/industrial product sales and marketing with after sales services i.e. Engineering & Tech. Support. Although the company is new, the appointed staffs are well experienced and able to handle most of the basic and some advanced systems. The office is well equipped with all modern communication systems and service facilities. It is centrally located in a prime commercial area in Dhaka, the Capital city of Bangladesh.
