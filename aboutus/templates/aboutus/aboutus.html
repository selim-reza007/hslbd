About Us

Healthcare Solutions Ltd. is a fully owned member of Ornate & Lyric Business Group. The company has been established to dedicate itself to healthcare products and services, involving import, sales, marketing, distribution and supply through business to business promotion & direct sales, tender participation in private and government Healthcare / Industrial procurement in Bangladesh. The company primarily specializing in Healthcare consumers Products and medical devices, industrial equipment, chemical and regents, and is ready to ensure high quality customer service and support, a very important key to success in this field. The company is managed by highly motivated professionals with expert knowledge and specialization in modern healthcare/industrial product sales and marketing with after sales services i.e. Engineering & Tech. Support. Although the company is new, the appointed staffs are well experienced and able to handle most of the basic and some advanced systems. The office is well equipped with all modern communication systems and service facilities. It is centrally located in a prime commercial area in Dhaka, the Capital city of Bangladesh. Our Sponsor Directors, already established in International trade, foresee the importance and growth of healthcare related industries in Bangladesh, its vast range and complexity. They have decidedly embarked upon this venture to contribute their best effort in realizing their outstanding vision for an unique healthcare supply/support organization in Bangladesh to meet the growing demands and also to fulfill their social commitment. It is therefore, the prime objective of this company is to emerge as a leading business house in this field within a short time. With a humble beginning in 1993-94, the Ornate & Lyric group has expanded its horizons gradually with a steady growth. Group's turnover in Bangladesh is more than US$ 70 million per annum with management staff strength of 500 personnel. Presently, the Group is active with more than 15 companies in Bangladesh and abroad dealing in areas of Import, sales & marketing, Indenting, and Manufacturing.
